Welcome to **Snippet AI** — your all-in-one platform that transforms long-form event recordings into short, engaging, and shareable micro-content. Whether you’re an event organizer, marketer, or content strategist, Snippet AI helps you **turn hours of video into moments that matter** — highlights, quotes, summaries, and visuals — all powered by AI.

## What is Snippet AI ?

**Snippet AI** is an advanced content repurposing tool built for the event industry.

It automatically detects key moments from your video or audio sessions, extracts the most engaging clips, and generates ready-to-share content such as:

- Short videos
- Key takeaways
- Speaker highlights
- Auto-generated summaries
- Social-ready visuals and thumbnails

With Snippet AI, you can give your event content a **second life** — perfect for social media, newsletters, microsites, and post-event engagement.

## How Snippet AI Works

The process is simple, automated, and powerful:

1. **Upload Your Content** — Add your session videos, audio, or documents.
2. **AI Analyzes Everything** — The platform identifies the most impactful segments based on speech patterns, topics, and audience energy.
3. **Snippets Are Created** — You get automatically edited short clips, smart visuals, and AI-written summaries.
4. **Review & Publish** — Edit, fine-tune, and share your micro-content instantly via your event microsite or social channels.

## System Requirements

To ensure a smooth experience with Snippet AI, make sure your system meets these minimum requirements:

| Requirement                | Details                                                                 |
| :------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Supported Browsers**     | Google Chrome (latest), Microsoft Edge, Safari (v14+), Firefox (latest) |
| **Internet Speed**         | Minimum 5 Mbps upload / 10 Mbps download recommended                    |
| **Supported File Formats** | MP4, MOV, AVI, MP3, WAV, PDF                                            |
| **Max Upload Size**        | 2 GB per file (Contact support for larger uploads)                      |
| **Device Compatibility**   | Desktop and Laptop browsers fully supported; Mobile upload in beta.     |
